How it works

          
        

        

          
01
You describe your situation
Not a form. Just write what's going on — the way you'd tell a friend.

          
02
You add your options
Name each choice. For each one, describe the best and worst case with a quick likelihood estimate.

          
03
We run 7 analyses
Expected value, reality check, sunk cost trap, evidence update, survivorship filter, smart sizing, and sensitivity testing.

          
04
You get a straight answer
Not charts and numbers — a clear recommendation with the reasoning behind it. Plus visuals that make it click.

          
05
You track what happens
Come back later, log the real outcome. Over time, Rational learns your patterns and helps you spot your blind spots.
