    How Rational works
    
          
        
    
        
    
          
    
            
    We add up the value of each choice
    
            
    For every option, we multiply each possible outcome by how likely it is, then add them up. The option with the highest total is usually the smartest pick.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We check the real-world numbers
    
            
    Most people overestimate their chances. If you think there's an 80% chance of success but the real-world average is 15%, we'll flag that gap.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We spot when you're stuck on the past
    
            
    If you've already spent a lot on one option, you might stick with it just because walking away feels like wasting what you've put in. We check for that.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We filter out success story noise
    
            
    Hearing about one person who succeeded can make something feel more likely than it really is. We remind you to look at the full picture, not just the winners.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We tell you how much to commit
    
            
    Even when you have a good bet, going all-in is risky. We calculate the smart portion of your budget to commit — enough to matter, not so much that it ruins you.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We test if your answer holds up
    
            
    What if your probability guesses are off by 20%? We let you drag sliders to see whether the best option changes. If it doesn't, you can trust your conclusion.
    
          
    
          
    
            
    We track how accurate you are
    
            
    Over time, you log what actually happened. We compare your predictions to reality and give you an accuracy score. The more you use Rational, the better you get at decisions.
