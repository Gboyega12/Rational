How it works

          
        

        

          
01
You describe your situation
Not a form. Just write what's going on — the way you'd tell a friend.

          
02
7 frameworks run on your problem
Not a chatbot opinion — structured analysis. Expected value math, base rate reality checks, sunk cost detection, Bayesian updating, survivorship bias filtering, Kelly Criterion sizing, and sensitivity testing.

          
03
Real numbers, cited sources
Every figure comes with a source — government statistics, industry data, published research. When we're uncertain, we say so.

          
04
One clear recommendation
Not "it depends." A specific answer with the math behind it, the blind spots you're missing, and exactly how much to commit.

          
05
You track what happens
Come back, log the real outcome. Over time, Rational learns your patterns and helps you spot your blind spots.
